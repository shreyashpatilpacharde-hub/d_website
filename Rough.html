Sahyadri Stadium

The Sahyadri Cricket Association Stadium features a magnificent open-air cricket ground surrounded by elliptical-shaped spectator stands. These stands are supported by strong raking beams and are divided into two levels – an upper tier and a lower tier. Together, they can accommodate approximately 45,000 spectators.

The elliptical design of the stands enhances the beauty of the stadium while ensuring excellent visibility for every spectator. As a result, fans can enjoy the match from any seat and fully immerse themselves in the excitement of cricket.

The large seating capacity creates an energetic atmosphere where supporters can enthusiastically cheer for their favorite teams and players.

The stadium has been designed with the goal of providing a world-class sporting experience. Combining modern facilities with innovative architecture, it serves as a memorable destination for cricket enthusiasts. Whether it is the thrilling action on the field or the excitement of the cheering crowd, the Sahyadri Cricket Association Stadium offers a unique and captivating environment for cricket matches.
