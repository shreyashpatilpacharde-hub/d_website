About

Cricket Maharashtra is a dedicated platform that embodies the spirit of cricket in the Maharashtra region. As enthusiasts and advocates of the sport, our mission is to foster a vibrant cricketing community, nurturing talent from grassroots to professional levels.

Through our comprehensive coverage, we strive to connect cricket enthusiasts, players, and stakeholders across Maharashtra. Our commitment extends beyond the boundary, aiming to instill a love for the game and contribute to the growth of cricket in the region. Join us in celebrating the rich cricketing heritage of Maharashtra and be part of a community that shares the passion for this beloved sport.
